How Our Robot Works

1

Vine Scanning

The robot uses advanced 3D vision to scan each vine, identifying branches, buds, and growth patterns with millimeter precision.

2

AI Analysis

Our proprietary algorithms analyze each vine's structure and health to determine optimal pruning points for maximum yield and quality.

3

Precision Pruning

Sterilized cutting tools make clean, angled cuts exactly where needed, minimizing disease risk and promoting healthy regrowth.

4

Data Collection

Each pruning session generates detailed health reports and growth predictions for your entire vineyard.
